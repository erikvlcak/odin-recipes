Chilli Con Carne

Description

This is my most favorite food. If done properly, it is super spicy. But I like spicy food, so thats why I enjoy eating Chilli Con Carne.

If you also want to shit flames, please keep on reading.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Red Beans
  • Corn
  • Spices
  • Chilli
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cook meat in large pot until it is cooked properly
  2. Mix spices in water and add to the pot with meat.
  3. Add corn, beans and chilli peppers.
  4. Cook for apporx. 15 more minutes.

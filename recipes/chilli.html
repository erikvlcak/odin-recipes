Chilli Con Carne

Description

This is a super-food.

And by super, I mean super HOT. If you also want to set your toilet on fire, please keep on reading.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Red Beans
  • Corn
  • Spices
  • Chilli
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cook meat in large pot until it is cooked properly.
  2. Mix spices in water and add to the pot with meat.
  3. Add corn, beans and chilli peppers.
  4. Cook for apporx. 15 more minutes.

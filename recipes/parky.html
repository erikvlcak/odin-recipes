Sausages with beans and cheese

Description

This meal contains the best ingredients present in this galaxy:

  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Sausages

If done properly, it will burn a hole into your intestines. If you want to suffer with me, here is how to do it!

Ingredients:

  • Sausages
  • Red Beans from a can
  • Cheese (sliced)
  • Chilli Peppers

Preparation:

  1. Put canned beans into medium-sized pot and heat up.
  2. Put water into medium-sized bowl and submerge sausages. Cook them properly.
  3. Transfer beans into glass plate and put sausages on top. And chilli.
  4. Cover sausages with slices of cheese and put into microwave for 8-10 minutes.

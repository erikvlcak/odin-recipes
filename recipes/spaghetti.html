Super Spicy Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti are a timeless classic.

But unlike spaghetti, we are not timeless - in fact, due to global warming, we might die pretty soon. So why not enjoy our lives while we still can and try out this food?

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced meat
  • Italian spices
  • Tomatoes
  • Chilli
  • Parmesan

Preparation steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti.
  2. In the meantime, cook meat on a hot pan.
  3. When the meat is done, add tomatoes, chilli and spices.
  4. Pour out the spaghetti water and add mix from the pan into pot with spaghetti.
  5. Mix properly and add parmesan just before serving.
